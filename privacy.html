Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 2025

1. Introduction

At Vista Restaurante, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This policy explains how we handle information when you visit our website and use our booking or contact forms.

2. Data We Collect

We only collect information that you voluntarily provide to us via our online forms, including:

  • Name and contact details (Email, Phone).
  • Reservation details (Date, Time, Number of Guests).
  • Any special requests or messages you send us.

3. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used strictly for:

  • Managing and confirming your table reservations.
  • Responding to your direct inquiries.
  • Improving our customer service.

4. Data Storage and Security

We use Formspree as our form processor. When you submit a form, your data is securely transferred and stored according to their industry-standard security protocols. We do not sell or share your data with third-party advertisers.

5. Cookies

We use basic cookies to improve your browsing experience and remember your preferences (such as your cookie consent choice). You can manage your cookie settings through your browser at any time.

6. Your Rights

Under GDPR, you have the right to access, correct, or request the deletion of your personal data. To exercise these rights, please contact us at info@vista.com.
